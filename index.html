SPARC

Space Physics Advance Research Community

The Space Physics Advance Research Community (SPARC Lab) is a team of physicists, PhD scholars, and Master's students at the Department of Physics, University of Mumbai. We focus on studying the Sun and its activities to better understand space weather. Our research looks into solar events like Coronal Mass Ejections and their effects on Earth’s space environment. Through our work, we aim to improve space weather forecasting, helping protect satellites, communication systems, and power grids. Join us as we explore the Sun and its impact on our planet’s space weather studies.
