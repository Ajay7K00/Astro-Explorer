Dr. Anil Narayan Raghav
Associate Professor | Space-weather Consultant

Dr. Anil Raghav is an Associate Professor in the Department of Physics at the University of Mumbai, with over 15 years of experience in space science research. An expert in interplanetary coronal mass ejections (ICMEs), solar wind dynamics, and space weather, he has published over 50+ peer-reviewed papers in renowned international journals.

Dr. Raghav has successfully led major research projects, supervised multiple Ph.D. students, and collaborated with prestigious institutes. Beyond academia, Dr. Raghav provides data-driven insights and consultancy services for industries impacted by space weather. His research continues to push the boundaries of understanding solar-terrestrial interactions, helping safeguard critical technologies from space weather disruptions.
