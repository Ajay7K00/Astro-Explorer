X-ray astronomy is a fascinating branch of astronomy that allows us to study the universe's most energetic phenomena. Unlike optical light, which is easily blocked by dust and gas, X-rays can penetrate these obstacles, revealing hidden celestial objects and processes. By analyzing the X-ray emission from these objects, we can gather valuable insights into their nature and behavior.
